Systematic: The New Single from King No-One

The song begins with a catchy bass rhythm followed by the introduction of synth sounds. The simple drum beat accompanies the bass perfectly and the tune is enhanced further by the addition of upbeat guitar riffs whilst singer Zach Lount displays an impressive vocal range. By incorporating an Arctic Monkeys-esque bass line, the song has been given a slight funk feel to it.
